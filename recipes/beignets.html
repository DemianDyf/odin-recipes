Beignets

Description

Beignets are deep-fried, soft, square pastries that are served warm with powdered sugar. This is a traditional New Orleans-style beignet recipe that's easy to make and can't be beat! Grab a caf√© au lait and you're set!

Ingredientes

  • Taspoon salt
  • Large Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup Shortening

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Dissolve yeast in warm water
  3. Mix in shortening
  4. Roll dough out on floured surface to 1/8-inch thick
  5. Heat oil in a deep pot to 360 degrees F (180 degrees C)
  6. Fry beignets in batches until browned and puffed
  7. Shake confectioners' sugar over hot beignets. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
